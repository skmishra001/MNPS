Welcome To Motilal Nehru Public school

Motilal Nehru Public School is a co-education English Medium school managed by the Motilal Nehru Public School managing committee and sponsored by the U.P. Sangh of Jamshedpur. The basic philosophy and the principles of Panditji form the guidelines of this institution. Panditji wanted the upliftment of India through Science and western education. Sri Jawaharlal’s upbringing education and culture are the manifestations of Pandit Motilal’s educational philosophy of life. The school will make all efforts to mould and cast children in the framework of above perception.

From Principal's Desk

Hello and welcome to our portal. We are daringly different, but true to traditions! To introduce my school to you – We are an organization open to new ideas and concepts. Our biggest strength is the ability to build confidence in the students. We constantly evolve-trying to integrate the modern while preserving values and traditions. A host of opportunities, both for the teachers and the taught exists at MNPS. Through various competitions we enable holistic development. The ‘WOW’ factor is all along focused upon. We do understand that in every child there is a ‘Gem’ wanting to be polished. Our organization is constantly bringing in new ideas and is not stagnant in its views or thoughts. With a view of embedding excellence in our school is to improve the quality of education, we are in the process of submitting an application each year for the JJI award for excellence in education. We have received commendation for institutionalizing excellence across our school. We welcome you to visit our website and familiarize yourselves with what we offer. – Ashu Tiwary, Principal – Motilal Nehru Public School
